以津真天 ITSUMADE

1334s Jianwu, every night there would be a strange voice from a bird shouting:"When is....Until when...."

People found out that it is a strange bird with a human face, with a body like a snake, two feet with sharp claws like swords, wings opens out a full five meters wide. It is said that the strange bird appeared during the plague year, the resentment of the dead that led to the appearance of such a strange bird.

般若 HANNYA

Hannya is a kind of demon that belongs to the spirit of resentment, which is the origin of a person's jealousy, causing their soul to leave their body while they are alive, and their soul can attack and even kill the person that they are jealous of.

青行灯 AOANDON

When someone tells a demon story, a demon will appear after they're finished.

It is said that when people play the game of 'A Hundred Demon Lights', they will lighten up a hundred lights and extinguish one for each story. When all the lights are gone, Aoandon will appear.
